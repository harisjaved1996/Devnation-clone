FAQS

Can I get accepted into the program even if I don't have a CS background?

Yes. You can get accepted in our program as long as you are a fast learner and show a passion for programming in your interview. Our program is designed for people from engineering, social sciences and humanities background!

What can I expect to be after DevNation?

After graduation, you will be a software engineer! You can easily get a job as a frontend engineer, backend engineer or a full-stack software engineer specifically in the web development. You will also be able to freelance as a web developer.

Will I get a certificate after graduation?

Yes - we will give you a certificate and also your program transcript that will show your progress and skills to your employers.

Will DevNation help me land a job?

Absolutely! Our career services go beyond the duration of the program. Our ultimate goal is your success and we do everything in our power to help you land a high-paying job.

I am a student - can I apply?

Absolutely - you can apply for our programs. You won't be able to get the "deferred tuition' option but we have scholarships of up to 50% that you can apply for.
